Ingredients

  1. Fresh hot dog bread
  2. Tomatoes
  3. Avocado
  4. Sausages

How to

  1. Wash your hands!
  2. Prepare your sausage as you liked, boiled or fried
  3. Cut your tomatoes in little squares, beware the knife!
  4. Open your avocado in half, extract all the avocado and crush it with a fork, make a coarse avocado pulp
  5. Add salt as you liked
  6. Open your bred put your sausage, then put some tomatoe and finish with a layer of avodado
  7. Finally add mayonaise or other sauces of your choice

    8. ENJOY!
