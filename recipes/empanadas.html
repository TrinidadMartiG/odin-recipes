Empanadas

One of the heavyweights of Chilean food

The one an only "Empanada de pino" is a traditional Chilean dish

with their tasty body, and delicious fillings will surprise you everytime

Ingredients

For the filling

  • Cow meat: 1 kg
  • Onion: 3 big ones
  • Flour: 2 tablespoon
  • Chili pepper: 2 teaspoon
  • Cumin: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Oil: 4 tablespoon
  • Salt & pepper
  • Cooked Eggs: 5

For the dough

  • Milk: 1 Cup
  • Hot water: 1 Cup
  • Salt
  • Flour: 1 kg
  • Yolk: 4
  • Butter: 180 g

How to

Making the filling

  1. fry in a pan the meat in hot oil, add chili, salt, cumin & pepper
  3. Add a bit of water and let it cook for 30 min
  4. Let the mix rest for 1 day

Making the dough

  1. Mix the flour with the water, and add salt
  2. Add the yolk to the mix in a continuum mixing motion
  3. Add the butter and keep mixing
  4. When you have a soft and elastic dough cut little balls
  5. stretch the dough making a big circle
  6. Put 2 Tablespoon of the filling you made the day before
  7. Close the dough folding it in half
  8. Then fold each side giving them it carasteristic square form
  9. Put them in the oven at 350Â°F for 30 min
  10. Eat them hot!
