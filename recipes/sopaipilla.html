Sopaipillas

Tea perfect match

Tipically on cold days you will have a little dinner with this ladies here in Chile

This is just another ace up one's slee. Easy to cook, and even easier to eat with any seasoning

Ingredients

  • Flour: 2 cups
  • Pumpkin: 1 cup cooked and ground
  • Butter: 2 tablespoon
  • Salt: 1 teaspoon
  • Oil

How to

  1. Wash your hands!
  2. Mix all ingredients untill get a dough
  3. Stretch your dough on a smooth surface, and cut circles with a cutting mold
  4. Open your avocado in half, extract all the avocado and crush it with a fork, make a coarse avocado pulp
  5. Heat your oil and drop the circles careffully!
  6. Fried them 3 minutes, take them out and let them rest in a absorbent surface
  7. Eat them as you want! Alone, with mayonaise, cheese, whatever you want!

    8. ENJOY!
